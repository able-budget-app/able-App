# Automating on-time payments

Never miss a payment again

Payment history is 35% of your score. One missed payment can cost 60 to 110 points. Fix is simple: automate everything.

Manual payments fail. Life gets busy. You forget. One week late and a decade of credit work vanishes in a single cycle.

Here's the foolproof system.

## Key points

- <strong>Autopay minimums on every card.</strong> Auto-pay the minimum on the due date. You're never late, even if you forget to log in.
- <strong>Manually pay the statement balance.</strong> Autopay protects your credit. Manual full payments protect your bank account from interest.
- <strong>Match due dates to pay dates.</strong> Most issuers let you change the due date. Line it up with when you have the most cash.
- <strong>Variable income? Pay weekly.</strong> Don't wait for the due date. Pay cards weekly as income comes in. Keeps utilization low, guarantees on-time status.
- <strong>Keep a buffer in checking.</strong> Autopay failures from insufficient funds = late payments. A small cushion prevents it.
