# Money is a skill you can learn

Financial literacy is a human right

Most people feel behind on money because nobody taught them. Not in school, not at home, not at work. You were expected to figure out taxes, investing, insurance, and debt on your own.

Money isn't a personality trait. It's not about how smart you are or how hard you work. It's a set of skills, and skills can be learned.

This course walks through the 7 Money Milestones. Your checklist for taking control. Tackle them well, not in order.

## Key points

- <strong>The 7 Milestones are your roadmap.</strong> Financial Education, Proper Protection, Emergency Fund, Debt Management, Cash Flow, Build Wealth, Protect Wealth. Every lesson ahead is one of these.
- <strong>Most financial pain comes from not knowing the rules.</strong> Once you know them, anxiety drops before the balance even moves.
- <strong>Time is your superpower.</strong> Older generations would pay to get back the years you have. Starting today with small amounts beats starting perfectly five years from now.
- <strong>You're not behind. You're starting.</strong> Starting today puts you ahead of everyone still avoiding it.

## Act now or pay later

Every financial decision lands in one of two columns: ACT NOW or PAY LATER. Learning this today is the ACT NOW column. Skipping isn't neutral. It sends the bill forward, with interest.
