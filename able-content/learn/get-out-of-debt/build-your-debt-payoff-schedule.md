# Build your debt payoff schedule

Your 90-day action plan

You have the theory. Time to build the plan. A 90-day starter schedule from drowning to moving.

Don't skip steps. Don't change the order. Each step makes the next one easier.

Work this for three months and you'll be in a fundamentally different place.

## Steps

**1. Week 1: List everything.** List every debt. Creditor, balance, minimum, rate, due date. You can't solve what you can't see.

**2. Week 2: Calculate your DTI.** Use the formula from Lesson 3. Write it at the top of the list. That's your starting score.

**3. Week 3: Pick your target.** Using snowball or avalanche, circle the one debt you'll attack first.

**4. Week 4: Build your buffer.** Open a separate savings account. Route 5 to 10% of every income into it. Goal: one month of bills.

**5. Month 2: Call for rate cuts.** Call every credit card and ask for a rate reduction. 30 minutes. Can save you thousands.

**6. Month 2: Start surge payments.** Let Able route surplus income to your target debt on good months.

**7. Month 3: Review progress.** Recalculate your DTI. Check how much principal you knocked off. Adjust if needed.

**8. Month 3: Lock it in.** The system should feel like routine by now. That's the win. Consistency beats intensity.
